Vegan Musrhoom Tacos

Description

Flavourful veggie tacos!

Recipe Credits:

  • Feasting at Home - Blog

Ingredients

  • 2 portobello mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 corn torillas, toasted in a dry pan
  • 14-ounce can refried beans
  • Optional: pickled onions, vegan queso, guacamole.

Marinade

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsps canned chipotle in adobo sauce (SAUCE ONLY)
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F.
  2. Mix the marinade ingredients together. Brush the mushrooms, peppers, and onion with the marinade and season them with salt.
  3. Roast for 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Heat the beans, adding additonal garnishes if desired.
  5. When ready to serve, warm the tortillas on a dry pan or over a gas burner. Assemble the tacos with any additonal toppings.
